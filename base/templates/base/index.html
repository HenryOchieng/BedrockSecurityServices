    About Us

    Bedrock Holdings Ltd was registered and started operations in 2002. We are a major private security services provider with assignments spread across the country.The company offers integrated security solutions manned and electronic security to companies, organizations, institutions, businesses and individuals.

    Our Mission

    To be the private security services provider of choice in Eastern and Central Africa through intelligence and resource sharing.

    Our Philosophy

    We believe in delivery of excellence and total fulfillment of clients' security and related needs, by strict adherence to integrity and loyalty.

    Our Vision

    To be the private security services provider of choice in Eastern and Central Africa through intelligence and resource sharing.

Our Uniformed certified security guards are trained to provide not just visual deterrent to unwanted element on site, but also to ensure prevention of incidents through keen eye for detail
